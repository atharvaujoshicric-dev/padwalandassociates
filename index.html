The Firm

Advocacy grounded in law,
fluent in technology.

Padwal & Associates is a Pune-based law firm built around a simple premise: as life and business move online, legal counsel has to keep pace. The firm is led by Adv. Shantanu Padwal, whose practice sits at the intersection of traditional litigation and emerging technology law.

Alongside a dedicated Cyber Law & Cybersecurity practice — covering the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, IT Act 2000 compliance, cybercrime litigation and data breach response — the firm represents individuals, families and businesses across Civil, Criminal, Corporate, Consumer and Family law matters.

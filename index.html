About the Firm

A practice rooted in diligence, defined by foresight.

Padwal & Associates is a full-service law firm built around a simple principle: clients deserve counsel that is both technically rigorous and genuinely accessible. Founding advocate Shantanu Padwal has built a practice spanning civil, criminal, corporate and family law — while developing a particular depth of expertise in Cyber Law, an area that now sits at the heart of the firm's identity.

That specialisation covers the Information Technology Act 2000, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, cybercrime litigation, data breach response and corporate cybersecurity advisory — equipping the firm to represent clients confidently as legal and digital risk increasingly intersect. Every other practice area is handled with the same meticulous, preventive approach.
